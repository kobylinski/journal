# research

The record of an investigation - options, feasibility, or a tool weighed - that ends in a
verdict. Default `status: confirmed` once the verdict is reached.

Worth capturing - include what applies, and anything else that matters:

- the question being settled
- the options considered, with the evidence that moved each up or down
- the verdict, and why
- what would change it

The point is that a later reader can trust the verdict without re-running the investigation. If
it is overturned later, that is a new entry with `supersedes`.
